# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, founders, tech leads, designers, and partners who need to quickly understand the visual and technical bar of a frontend engineer.

## Product Purpose

One-page portfolio for presenting presence, repertoire, and frontend execution. Success means the visitor remembers the site, trusts the technical craft, and opens a conversation or project.

## Brand Personality

Brutalist, precise, sigilist. The voice should sound direct, technical, and authored, with high visual energy without losing readability.

## Anti-references

It should not feel like a generic SaaS template, a personality-free minimal portfolio, a page dominated by repeated cards and soft shadows, or an AI landing page with purple gradients, glassmorphism, or decorative illustrations.

## Design Principles

- Show technical command through the interface itself.
- Use motion as structural material, not ornament.
- Keep the ZINC palette restricted and turn limitation into identity.
- Prioritize asymmetric, dense, memorable layouts.
- Write short, sharp English copy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA, high contrast, visible focus, and no dependence on animation alone to communicate content. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` where motion is not the core visual material.
